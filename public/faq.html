Frequently Asked Questions

How does FitnessClass create personalized workouts?

Our AI system analyzes multiple factors including your equipment availability, age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, and experience level to create customized workout plans. The system continuously learns from your progress to provide increasingly tailored recommendations.

Why use 3D demonstrations instead of videos?

Our WebGL-rendered 3D demonstrations allow you to view exercises from any angle without interrupting your audio. This means you can keep listening to your music or podcasts while checking proper form, providing a seamless workout experience.

How can I track my progress?

FitnessClass provides an intuitive logging system where you can record your workouts, track progress over time, and easily revisit previous sessions. This helps maintain consistency and monitor your fitness journey.

Is the AI recommendation system accurate?

While our AI system uses advanced algorithms to provide personalized recommendations, it's important to listen to your body and adjust workouts as needed. The system learns from your feedback to improve recommendations over time.

Can I use FitnessClass without equipment?

Yes! Our AI system takes into account your available equipment and can create effective bodyweight workouts when no equipment is available.
